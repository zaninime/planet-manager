Control your ELOS Planet lamp. Choose your preferred daily theme and customize the sunset and night colors. With the brand-new interface you can access all the function in a more organized and simplified way.

The app is compatible with all the lamps from the series "ELOS Planet". Be sure to connect your ELOS WiFish first, as the connections take place over Wi-Fi!

Supported models:
 - PlanetPRO
 - PlanetCompact
 - PlanetStella

Note: some old firmwares loaded on pre-2016 lamps contain a bug related to the timings setup. The app automatically tries to mitigate it, but unfortunately the process isn't reliable on every old model.
